As per IEEE norms and ISI standards the earthing resistance should be as minimum as possible for any application. But still we can refer to some standards for checking whether the earth resistance is in permissible limits or not. Earthing resistance should not exceed the below limits as per their application:

S.no Application Maximum Earthing Resistance
1 Mega Power Station 0.5 Ω
2 Small Power Station 1.0 Ω
3 Large scale industry 2.0 Ω
4 Commercial Purpose 2.5 Ω
5 All Other 5.0 Ω
